SMALL GROUP ONE: "STEP IN"

Life / Intro Questions

  • What’s one good and one bad thing that happened at school this week?
  • What was your favorite part about opening night of Fusion?

Connection Questions

  • What’s something you’ve stepped into this school year that you wish you wouldn’t have?
  • How have you stepped into your relationship with God?

Sermon in a Sentence

  • Every Journey Begins With A Single Step.

Scripture

  • Luke 4:38-40 Jesus left the synagogue and went to the home of Simon. Now Simon’s mother-in-law was suffering from a high fever, and they asked Jesus to help her. 39So he bent over her and rebuked the fever, and it left her. She got up at once and began to wait on them. 40At sunset, the people brought to Jesus all who had various kinds of sickness, and laying his hands on each one, he healed them.

Discussion Questions

  • If you had a family member that was sick, what would you do?
  • How would you respond if you saw someone healed instantaneously?
  • Would you bring people or the miracle or would you keep it for yourself?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

  • How are you going to step into tough conversations?
  • How are you going to step in and share the gospel?

Take It Out

  • Wake Up Time
  • Next Day Schedule

"Step In" Car Questions

  • What are some things that you are committing to this year?
  • What are some disciplines you’ll participate in this year and what will change because of it?
  • How hungry are you for God?
  • What obstacles and Distractions need to be removed from your life?
  • Are you growing in your knowledge of Jesus and His teachings? (1 Chronicles 16:11)
  • When did you choose to follow Jesus?
  • How has following Jesus given you an abundant life?
  • What would it look like for you to go ALL IN for Jesus?
  • What happened when Peter followed at a distance? (John 18:15-27)

Salvation Follow-Up

Did any of your students make a decision to follow Jesus this weekend? If so, make sure to continue to have some follow-up conversations with them this weekend about their decision, as you encourage them and discuss any questions they may have. In those conversations, please also talk about baptism with them. If they would like to be baptized this weekend, please reach out to their parents to get permission first and let your coach know once the parents have given permission.
