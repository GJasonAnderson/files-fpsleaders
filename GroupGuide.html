At the Movies Week 1

Life / Intro Questions (Choose one or two)

  • High School Groups - What movie were the clips from? Do you like that movie?
  • Middle School Groups - Take a guess on what different movies they’re going to play throughout the series.

Connection Question

  • Have you ever felt unqualified? Maybe those around you are better equipped for something than you? Share a story of when this happened.

Sermon in a Sentence

  • God does not call the equipped, he equips the called.

Scripture

  • 2 Corinthians 3:5 It is not that we think we are qualified to do anything on our own. Our qualification comes from God.
  • What is Jesus saying in this scripture?

Discussion Questions

  • Do you ever not feel qualified for something? Maybe because you’re too young, uneducated, and too dependent? Give examples.
  • How many of you play sports or are involved in something with a group? How often do you find yourself following your teammates more than God? Explain a time.
  • Do you think that God can call you to do great things before you’re equipped with the ability to do those great things?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

  • Right now in your life, what do you think God has called you to do?
  • How can you faithfully trust that God will equip you so you can live out His calling this week?

Take It Out

  • Close small group out with prayer requests.
  • After prayer, remind them of THE WEEKEND and any upcoming group events!
