Week 4

Life / Intro Questions (Choose one or two)

  • What was a high point of your week and a low point of your week?
  • Would you rather receive a gift or give a gift to someone?

Connection Question

  • Has there ever been a time where you got a gift for someone and they responded poorly? Explain.

Sermon in a Sentence

  • We worship God because of what he did for us.

Scripture

  • Romans 12:1 NLT “And so, dear brothers and sisters, I plead with you to give your bodies to God because of all he has done for you. Let them be a living and holy sacrifice—the kind he will find acceptable. This is truly the way to worship him.”
  • What is Jesus saying in this scripture?

Discussion Questions

  • Personally, how do you see the chains of sin hurting your life? It could be ruining your family, relationships, or yourself.
  • How do you believe God can help take those chains of sin away from you so you can have a strong relationship with Jesus?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

  • The way to truly worship God is to live for Him. What things do you do in your life that are not acceptable in the eyes of God?
  • How do you think you can change that?

Take It Out

  • Close small group out with prayer requests.
  • After prayer, be sure to remind them of Movement and THE WEEKEND!
