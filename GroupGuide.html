Week 2 - The Fire

Life / Intro Questions

  • What are your favorite shows to watch on Netflix?
  • How did you do last week making steps towards restoration in your life?

Connection Question

  • Share a story about a time where you saw something you couldn’t explain. Maybe it was a miracle or a strange phenomenon. What happened in that event and how did it change you?

Sermon in a Sentence

  • We do the natural, and God does the supernatural.

Scripture

  • 2 Chronicles 7:12-14 12Then one night the Lord appeared to Solomon and said, “I have heard your prayer and have chosen this Temple as the place for making sacrifices. At times I might shut up the heavens so that no rain falls, or command grasshoppers to devour your crops, or send plagues among you. Then if my people who are called by my name will humble themselves and pray and seek my face and turn from their wicked ways, I will hear from heaven and will forgive their sins and restore their land.
  • What is Jesus saying in this scripture?

Discussion Questions

  • How often do you find yourself being prideful in a situation? How do you think that pride can negatively affect the situation?
  • How often do you find yourself praying as a last resort, not a first response to trouble in your life? Why is that?
  • If comfortable sharing, is there anyone in this group that has not humbled themselves before the Lord, repented of their sins and asked Jesus into their life?

Life Application

We want students walking away with 1 or 2 things they are going to do SOON to apply what they discussed.

  • How are some ways this week you can humble yourself for God so He can do great things through you?

Baptism Follow-Up

  • Did you make a decision to follow Jesus for the first time at Fusion? Or maybe even tonight?
  • Have you made that decision sometime in the past but you've never gone public by being baptized?

Baptism is our first official act of obedience as a new Christian and a super easy way to tell the world about your decision. Encourage your students to "seal the deal" and get baptized if they are a follower of Jesus!

Take It Out

  • Close small group out with prayer requests.
  • After prayer, remind them of THE WEEKEND and any upcoming group events!
